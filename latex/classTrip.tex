\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contains information about a \mbox{\hyperlink{classTrip}{Trip}} from a \mbox{\hyperlink{classUser}{User}}. 

Each trip has a destination name, a time period (two \mbox{\hyperlink{classDate}{Date}} objects), rating (1-\/5), a \mbox{\hyperlink{classComment}{Comment}} object and a list of photo filenames. The comment has the username of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classUser}{User}} who made the visit, allowing identification later on.

The comment can be empty, as well as the photo filenames list. The beginning date can match the ending date for a trip period of a single day.
